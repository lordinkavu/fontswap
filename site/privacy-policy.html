We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a secure user experience. This privacy policy explains our practices regarding your information and how we protect your privacy.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Fontswap does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information. The extension operates entirely on your device without sending any information to external servers.

2. Local Storage of Preferences

Fontswap stores your font preferences locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This information is used solely to remember your font choices and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. Permissions

Fontswap requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • activeTab: Allows the extension to access the currently active tab to apply the selected font.
  • storage: Allows the extension to store your font preferences locally on your device.
These permissions are used solely for the purpose of providing the core functionality of the extension and do not result in any data being collected or transmitted.

4. No Third-Party Services

Fontswap does not integrate with any third-party services for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose that might involve data collection.

5. Children's Privacy

Fontswap is safe for users of all ages as it does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via Email.
